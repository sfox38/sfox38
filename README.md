## Vibe Coding and AI Policy
Full disclosure: yes, I vibe code. I began my career as a professional developer over 40 years ago and spent perhaps too many years in the industry. I approach vibe coding with the same instincts and understanding I would apply if I were writing every line manually. This workflow allows me to quickly get up to speed on projects that would otherwise require deep mastery of a specific platform or API. You won't find "AI slop" here; I work hard to produce concise, fully-featured code and I am never averse to manual debugging when necessary.

Regarding my music - all compositions and recordings are 100% human-made. There is no AI involved in the writing or production. While I am not inherently against the technology, I choose not to use it for my music projects at this time. However, some of my music videos are fully or partially AI-generated.
